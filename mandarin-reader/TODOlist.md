# 读中文 — Mandarin Reader Next steps?


## Needs
I want to use this app to read chinese novels. 

This means that: 

- there are a number of characters that I already know. I could input them one by one or import them from pleco (card of specific categories)

- I have the novels in chapters online on a web page. I can copy paste them over chapter by chapter (or even paragraph by paragraph) as I read but it could be convenient to have extra features like: import from epub format, have the texts be linked as a 'book' in my app and the highlighted characters are by book instead of by texts


## Main needed improvements

- book vocabulary is too difficult. I need a dictionnary
- UI: pinyin is cut off 
- book as a concept: container of texts that are ordered. 
    - Names highlighted per book (not text). 
    - Continue reading — opens the book directly to where you left off
        - Reading progress tracked per book (which chapter, roughly where in it)
- 

## Could be nice
- Characters: import/export known characters to PLECO
- Character frequency per book — before you start a novel, scan it and show you which characters appear most that aren't in your known list. Lets you pre-study the 20 most common unknown characters before starting, which makes the reading experience much smoother.
- Reading stats — characters read, time spent, pinyin lookups over time, which characters you've added to your known list


## Not usefull for me (but could be good for others)
- Characters: HSK level import
- EPUB import 
- URL import: you paste a chapter URL, the app fetches the page, strips the HTML, and extracts the Chinese text
- Copy-paste with auto-chapter detection
